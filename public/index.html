How's that novel of yours coming?

Now there's no excuse.

Novlr is currently in private beta with 200 beta testers and counting...

You're writing a novel. That makes you brave, committed and perhaps a touch foolish.

Why? Because it's hard.

So we've decided to help by taking care of the formatting, backups, word counts etc and letting you concentrate on those pesky, slippery little words.

You won't lose work. Ever.

We've all felt the pain of loss. Pain should generally be avoided. So we save everything, all the time. You don't need to think about it.

Your novel is always with you.

If you've got the internet, you've got Novlr. Your phone, a computer at work, your mum's ipad, that dodgy internet cafe - they all work.

Challenge yourself

Set yourself a goal. Then once you've finished that doodle of a pigeon, set yourself a writing goal. Track your progress and celebrate wins.

Tell us what you need...

We're here to solve writing problems. What frustrates you? We've got lots of ideas but tell us what's important and we'll make it happen.
