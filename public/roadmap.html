Novlr have big plans. We want you to be a part of them

Novlr is just starting out and you will be able to say you were there right a the beginning of that journey. Once the first beta release is out for testing we will be in constant development mode. Continually improving and updating Novlr based on what you, its users, tell us.

Beta launch

Our first release! We want to get a simple working version of Novlr out to you as soon as possible. It will have the following features

  • Simple, beautiful editing interface
  • Secure, stable backend with regular backups
  • Chapters
  • Perpetual saving
  • Export to to .doc and .pdf
  • Real time word count for chapter and novel
  • Reorder chapters
  • Tablet-friendly

Release 2: The bonus feature round

In this release we will be looking at getting you some extra features that help making writing easier. Including the following but this is open to what you tell us you want. Novlr is your writing tool.

  • Motivational tools
  • Character profiles
  • Accounts settings
  • Search capabilities

Future releases

Future releases will be planned as they get nearer. We have big ideas for Novlr including offline editing, multiple novels, editor collaboration and sharing, tagging chapters for quick reference tools, notes/ideas, exporting to ebook, narrative/plot tools, version tools (eg. draft, final, finalFINAL, definitelyfinalFINAL3)

Hopefully you like what you see. We are writers making this for writers - call it our version of procrastination (the house was already clean) - because we want to give YOU no more excuses.

Feedback welcome and encouraged!
