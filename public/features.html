Novlr makes novel writing simple

We have tried writing novels on all sorts of different platforms and just felt none of them did it well enough. So, we're trying to do it better. We want to make it the easiest place to do it. Writing a novel is hard enough. Why spend time worrying about:

Find it difficult to focus? Not anymore.

Novlr will put the focus on your words, stripping back the options and giving you a nice clean space to write. It'll just feel nice to write on it.

You won't lose work. Ever.

Novlr will save your work every couple of seconds so you don't even have to think about it. No more of the nightmares we all recognise.

Forgot your laptop? Not a problem

Novlr is web based, so you can access it from any computer, tablet, or phone with internet access. You don't need to carry it with you anymore.

Keep changing your mind? Change is good.

It takes a long time to get a novel right. We're making chapter organisation simple so you can change things around easily, and quickly add new ones.

Bring your characters to life. They deserve it.

No change in circumstances can repair a defect in character. So said Ralph Waldo Emerson. Novlr agrees and we're giving each character a profile page: take notes; upload images and videos: give them a back story.

Find it hard to keep motivated? Let us help.

Sometimes we all need a bit of carrot or stick ot get words on the page. Set yourself word count targets and setup reminder emails. We'll also celebrate each goal you achieve with you (maybe even with animated gifs).

Needing a second opinion? Just ask.

It can be tempting to keep your words close to your chest, but feedback is so valuable. Share chapters, or your whole book, with friends and let them comment directly.

Hate it when you get locked in? So do we.

We want you to use Novlr because it's the best tool, not because we've locked you in. So, you'll be able to export your work at any time into a range of formats - Word, Pdf etc.
